Text labels
Add labels to your progress bars by placing text within the .progress-bar. * We highly recommend adding more height to your progress bar so text labels fit perfectly.
50%
 <div class="progress h-4">
    <div class="progress-bar h-4 w-6/12">50%</div>
</div>
